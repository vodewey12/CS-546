A story of mine

I Once Was Casted as The Back-up Peter Pan to the Play Neverland

When I was in 8th grade, I wanted to try something different. Movies was a big part of my life, and as an immigrant, it was a useful way for me to learn english quickly, and efficiently. Not only that but I enjoyed that fantasy filled adrenaline that came from watching movies. From superheroes to horror flicks, I pretty much every movie from 1980s to the early 2000s (not actually true). Anyways, there was a play production for our middle school called Neverland, a story about a couple of kids being taken to Neverland with Peter Pan in order to help him, and Tinker Bell, fight off his arch-nemesis, Captain Hook.

I was originally going to be casted as the 3rd tree and although it was a small role, I wanted that 3rd tree role. It was quickly realized from the producers of the play, my teachers, that I was passionate about being a tree, so they let me be the back up, to the back up, of Peter Pan. So in a way, I went from the 3rd tree, to the 3rd Peter Pan. Once again, I didn't mind it because I still go to be a tree, but if the case was to ever happen to the other two Peter Pan, I would get to take over the show and be the main star.

Sadly, this never happened as both Peter Pan showed up, and I eneded playing the 3rd tree after all. I did quite a good job and was one hell of a 3rd tree!
